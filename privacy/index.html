Privacy Policy

System Boundary Reference: Zero-Retention Matrix

1. Core Architecture Blueprint

Illustrious is a decoupled, internal administrative spatial orchestration interface. The entire architecture operates under a strict Zero-Retention Policy. No data passing through this gateway is compiled, parsed for analytics, or preserved within any persistent database owned or maintained by Illustrious.

2. Token & Credential Handling

All Google Cloud Platform (GCP) credentials, Client Secrets, and OAuth2 access or refresh tokens are handled under complete individual encapsulation:

  • Local Environments: Configuration structures are maintained strictly within your machine's global profile footprint (~/.credentials/).
  • Cloud Handshakes: Short-lived tokens traded during the OAuth gateway sequence exist purely within the transient runtime execution memory of your micro-services. They are never transmitted back to an external telemetry hub.

3. Third-Party Interfaces

This system directly interfaces with Google Cloud Platform APIs to allocate serverless functions and compute clusters. Your interaction with those underlying modules is governed explicitly by the Google Cloud Privacy Agreement. Illustrious does not share, sell, or disclose tracking arrays to any third-party marketing brokers.

4. Modifications & Audit

Because this platform is completely containerized and controlled at your own endpoint, this policy remains immutable across your system allocations. For compliance queries, inspect your local deployment logs via the administrative wizard console.
